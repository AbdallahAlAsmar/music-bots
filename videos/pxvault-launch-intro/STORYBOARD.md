## Video direction

- Palette system: use `frame.md` roles with emerald accent as the primary action/highlight color and dark canvas for depth.
- Motion grammar: smooth long-tail reveals paced to narration; no front-loaded dumps.
- Rhythm: Frame 1 and Frame 2 reveal progressively; Frame 3 is a calm held CTA.
- Negative list: avoid browser chrome, cursor UI clutter, and over-busy particle motion.
